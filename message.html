Ronnie,

There's a particular kind of feeling that sneaks up on me in the middle of a completely normal day, not fireworks, not a grand moment, just this quiet, certain knowing that we have each other at all times. It's calming! That's what made me write this. The whole messy, beautiful, ridiculous journey we've been on together. There's this thing about us that we keep coming back to: we move through life like we're in each other's orbit. It doesn't matter if we're on top of the world or completely in the weeds, we always seem to find our way back to each other. No dramatic recalibration needed. No GPS required. You are just... my center. My constant. And I don't think I'll ever be able to fully articulate how much that means to me.

You are my love, yes, but more than that, you're genuinely my favorite person to be alive with. My first choice for my morning streaks and late-night spirals. The person I want to yap to, not just the big news but also my intrussive cray cray thoughts. I love that we didn't just fall into this ...... we keep choosing it, every single day. That's not something I take lightly. That's everything.

I want you to know how much I see you. The way you show up for me — steady and sure, in the grand gestures and in the ten thousand little ways that never make the highlight reel — it makes me feel known in the deepest way. You make me feel like I matter, even on the days I'm not sure I'm giving you much to work with.

And then there's your drive. Watching you go after the things you want, building something real out of sheer will and focus, it genuinely takes my breath away. I don't say this lightly: I believe in you with everything I have. Not the "of course I'll be supportive" kind of belief, but the loud, certain, won't-stop-talking-about-you kind. I am so proud of you. And I am so lucky to have a front-row seat to all of it. Thank you for being exactly who you are. You are my home, my heart and honestly ...... my favorite plot twist.

I love you always,

~ Jo ~
